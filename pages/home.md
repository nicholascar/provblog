## Home
ProvBlog is, perhaps unsurprisingly, a blog all about provenance! It addresses all provenance-related issues from provenance representation, organisational provenance policy, related issues in computer science and tooling.

This blog is maintained by [Nicholas Car](/page/about) who is involved with several provenance initiatives including the [Research Data Alliance's Provenance Patterns Working Group](https://www.rd-alliance.org/groups/provenance-patterns-wg).

See the [About Page](/page/about) for much more info.
